TERMS & CONDITIONS

Billing

Flexible billing options to meet your business needs.

-Credit card on file.
-Weekly billing by individual customer, department or account.
-Billing can be E-mailed for your convenience.

Long Ridge Limousine accepts
-American Express
-Dinners Club
-Discover
-Visa
-Master Card

A service fee of 20% will be added to the fare. Stops and waiting time will be itemized on the invoice.

Wait Time

-1 hour free for international flights.
-1/2 hour free for domestic flights.
-15 minutes free at office or residential pickups.

Waiting time will be billed at $45 an hour, on 15 minute increments.
Long Ridge monitors and checks flights on a regular basis, and dispatches cars accordingly.
Train schedules and private jets cannot be monitored, therefore billing begins at the scheduled pickup time.

Additional Charges

-Stops in town add $10.
-Stops out of town add $15.
-For trips below 34th St. add $10 to base.
-Major holidays add $25.


Cancellation Policy and No Shows

Cancellations must be made one hour in advance for local pickups (Stamford, CT). Three hours in advance for out of town pickups. Trips not canceled in accordance with this policy will be subject to charges.


Holiday Surcharges

$25 will be added to the invoice for the following holidays:
-New Years
-President's Day
-Memorial Day
-Fourth of July
-Labor Day
-Thanksgiving
-Christmas

Hourly Rates

For trips within the vicinity of Stamford, CT, there is a three hour minimum. All others have a five hour minimum. Hourly rates are calculated from the time the car leaves the garage in Stamford, CT until it returns.
